## 3Q results: Rev/EBITDA miss but ARPU grows & commitment to a better 4Q. Buy.
**Reiterate Rating: BUY | PO: 2,300 IDR | Price: 1,905 IDR**

### 3Q25 misses slightly but FY guidance maintained
Indosat’s 3Q revenue/EBITDA slightly missed our estimates by 2%/6% on weaker MIDI revenue growth vs. expectation, and higher G&A costs, driven by professional fees. Net income also missed by 2% (+9% yoy/+22% qoq) on EBITDA miss, partially offset by lower depreciation and tax outlay. Cost of services was in-line with our estimate, but higher sequentially (+8% yoy/+4% qoq) mainly on higher installation costs – mgmt. shared is related to higher MIDI/VAS revenues. While 9M25 revenue/EBITDA are tracking -2%/-3% yoy - mgmt. maintained FY25 EBITDA low single digit growth guidance – which we believe is achievable but translates to a strong 4Q with low double digit qoq EBITDA growth. We are building revenue/EBITDA growth of 6%/11% qoq in 4Q, to drive FY25 flat revenues/1% yoy EBITDA growth. Operating highlights: 1) Blended ARPU grew 4% qoq/8% yoy to IDR40K 2) Subscribers declined slightly with SIM consolidation impact on prepaid; 3) Data traffic grew 11% yoy /5% qoq

### GPUaaS revenues + cellular repair should drive 4Q growth
Mgmt. is confident of achieving their target of USD30-35mn (IDR500-550bn) revenue contribution from the GPUaaS for the FY, with majority coming in 4Q, and they expect FY26 contribution at USD65-70bn, with c. 20 customer contracts. Mgmt. is also confident on strong cellular recovery to continue in 4Q – with lower value customers seem optimizing, and good turnaround on the postpaid side. Given this is a higher margin business, the strong cellular growth along with continued cost optimization is expected to drive margin growth in 4Q.

### Reit. Buy – upside potential outweighs negatives
While 3Q nos. slightly missed our estimates, we believe key positives ahead are i) lower competition and more stable macro and ii) further ARPU growth expected in 4Q with clean-up of lower value plans and subs. We believe the high likelihood of management delivering on their guidance is also a positive for the share price – and maintain our Buy rating with an unchanged PO of IDR2,300. We slightly tweak our numbers for ISAT post results, for slightly lower FY margins, post a 3Q miss, we cut our FY25-27E EPS by 1% on average, but PO remains unchanged as we roll-forward our DCF.

### Price objective basis & risk

#### Indosat-Hutch (PTITF)
Our price objective of Rp2,300 is set using a Discounted Cash Flow Method (DCF). Assumptions in our DCF are driven by a risk free rate of 7.0%, a beta of 1.1x, to derive a WACC of 14.3% and we use a 2.0% terminal growth rate.

Downside risks to our price objective are: 1) material pick-up in competitive intensity, resulting in weaker-than-expected revenue, EBITDA, and earnings growth, and 2) weaker uptake of new initiatives such as GPUaaS offering
